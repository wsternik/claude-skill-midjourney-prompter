# Genre Templates & Example Prompts

## Cinematic Realism

**Template:**
```
[Scene description], [cinematographic technique], [lighting], [film stock/lens] --ar 21:9 --s 100-200 --v 7
```

**Examples:**
```
A lone figure walking through a rain-soaked Tokyo alley at night,
neon signs reflecting in puddles, Blade Runner atmosphere,
anamorphic lens flare, shot on 35mm Kodak Vision3 500T
--ar 21:9 --s 150 --v 7
```

```
An abandoned grand ballroom with sunlight streaming through broken windows,
dust motes floating in the air, overgrown with ivy,
Roger Deakins cinematography, natural volumetric light
--ar 21:9 --s 200 --v 7
```

---

## Portrait Photography

**Template:**
```
[Subject description], [expression/pose], [lighting setup], [camera/lens], [background] --ar 2:3 or 4:5 --s 50-150
```

**Examples:**
```
A weathered jazz musician in his 70s with deep smile lines,
holding a brass saxophone, contemplative expression,
Rembrandt lighting with warm tungsten fill,
shot on Hasselblad X2D, 80mm f/2.8, shallow depth of field,
dark studio background
--ar 2:3 --s 100 --v 7
```

```
Young woman with freckles and red curly hair,
candid laughter, golden hour sunlight through window,
shot on Fujifilm GFX, natural skin tones,
soft bokeh background of a cozy café
--ar 4:5 --s 80 --v 7
```

## Product Photography

**Template:**
```
[Product description], [surface/setting], [lighting rig], [camera details] --ar [varies] --s 50-100 --style raw
```

**Examples:**
```
A minimalist ceramic coffee mug in matte sage green,
on a light oak table with morning light from the left,
clean white background, product photography,
shot on Phase One, 120mm macro, f/11
--ar 1:1 --s 50 --style raw --v 7
```

```
Premium wireless headphones in matte black,
floating against deep navy gradient background,
three-point studio lighting with soft rim light,
editorial product photography for luxury brand
--ar 4:5 --s 80 --v 7
```

## Fantasy & Sci-Fi

**Template:**
```
[World/scene description], [atmospheric details], [style reference], [mood] --ar 16:9 --s 200-500
```

**Examples:**

A massive crystalline citadel floating above cloud banks at twilight,

An ancient forest temple reclaimed by nature,

## Anime with Niji 7

**Template:**
```
[Character description], [action/pose], [setting], [style cues] --niji 7 --ar [varies] --s [varies]
```

**Examples:**
```
A determined young mage with crimson hair and emerald eyes,
casting fire magic with both hands raised,
intense expression, ancient library filled with floating books,
dramatic angle from below
--niji 7 --ar 2:3 --s 150
```

```
Two rival swordswomen clashing blades on a moonlit rooftop,
cherry blossom petals swirling around them,
dynamic action composition, detailed clothing physics,
cool blue and warm pink color contrast
--niji 7 --ar 16:9 --s 200
```

## Architecture

**Template:**
```
[Building style and materials], [setting/context], [time of day], [perspective], [photographic style] --ar 16:9 or 3:2 --s 100-200
```

**Examples:**
```
A brutalist concrete library with dramatic cantilevers,
surrounded by a serene reflecting pool,
late afternoon golden light casting geometric shadows,
shot from a low angle emphasizing mass and form,
architectural photography by Ezra Stoller
--ar 3:2 --s 150 --v 7
```

## Abstract & Experimental

Use fewer tokens, higher chaos/weird, focus on mood/color/texture.

**Examples:**
```
Liquid mercury and sapphire, cosmic entropy
--ar 1:1 --s 600 --c 80 --w 500 --v 7
```

```
The sound of rain translated into geometry,
synesthetic visualization, deep indigo and gold
--ar 1:1 --s 800 --c 50 --w 200 --v 7
```
